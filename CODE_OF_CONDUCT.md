# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

LLM Guardian is built to be useful, welcoming, and safe for everyone. We expect all
contributors, maintainers, and community members to help create an environment that is
respectful, collaborative, and free from harassment.

Participation in this project means treating other people with professionalism, empathy,
and patience, even when you strongly disagree.

## Expected behavior

When participating in this project, please:

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other community spaces.
- Give constructive, actionable feedback focused on the work rather than the person.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and stay open to correction.
- Respect different viewpoints, experience levels, identities, and communication styles.
- Protect privacy and security, including avoiding the sharing of secrets or personal data.
- Help keep the project useful by staying on topic and collaborating in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in this community:

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, threats, or hateful conduct of any kind.
- Personal attacks, insults, mocking, or deliberately inflammatory comments.
- Sexualized language, unwelcome advances, or inappropriate imagery.
- Doxxing, sharing private information, or exposing credentials or sensitive data.
- Trolling, bad-faith argumentation, or sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Spam, impersonation, or deliberately misleading maintainers or contributors.
- Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern or participates in an investigation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Code review comments and commit discussions
- Documentation, examples, and community support spaces
- Any public or private communication when someone is representing this project

## Reporting concerns

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, contact the
maintainers privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile contact options or
another private maintainer channel if one is provided.

When making a report, include:

- What happened
- Where it happened
- Links, screenshots, or other supporting context if available
- Whether there is any immediate safety or privacy risk

Please avoid filing public issues for Code of Conduct reports unless you are explicitly
asked to do so by a maintainer.

If the report involves a maintainer, contact another maintainer or the repository owner
through a private channel.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Project maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate to protect the
community and the project, including:

- Removing or editing harmful content
- Closing discussions or locking threads
- Rejecting contributions
- Issuing warnings
- Temporarily suspending participation
- Permanently banning repeat or severe offenders

Maintainers will aim to review reports promptly, handle them fairly, and keep shared
details limited to people who need them to respond appropriately.

## Enforcement guidelines

Possible responses include:

1. **Correction** for minor conduct issues or misunderstandings.
2. **Warning** for repeated or more serious misconduct.
3. **Temporary restriction** from project interaction spaces.
4. **Permanent removal** from the community for severe or repeated violations.

## Project values

We want LLM Guardian to be known for:

- Clear, respectful technical discussion
- High trust and low drama
- Inclusive collaboration
- Security-minded, professional open-source development

Thank you for helping keep the community constructive and welcoming.
